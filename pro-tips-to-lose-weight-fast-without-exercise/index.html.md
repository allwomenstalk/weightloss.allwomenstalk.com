# 5 Pro Tips to Lose Weight Fast without Exercise ...

There ARE ways to lose weight without exercise. Watch the video!

## 1. Distractions

Have your meals without distractions. For example, no television! When distracted, you are going to mindlessly eat, not realizing how much you are consuming.

## 2. Speed

Eat slowly! If you eat fast, you are going to be thinking "Is that all?"

## 3. Water

Sometimes what may seem like hunger is your body crying out for water!

## 4. Sugar

Avoid sugary drinks as they are empty calories.

## 5. Protein

Makes one feel fuller.

Do all of these without exercise and you are going to see results!

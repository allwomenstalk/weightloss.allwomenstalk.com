# Girls Guide How to Use Your Brain to Slim down ...

Did you know that your brain can be a powerful tool to help you to lose weight and achieve your goals? But this is only the case if you focus on this. If you fail to have focus, your brain can also cause you to fail your weight loss plan and have you giving in to temptation. Simple choices like creating habits, thinking twice before your food choices, establishing a routine and even staying positive can help you to slim down. Allow mind over matter to help you to achieve your weight loss goals. If you are ready to start thinking thin, follow these tips:

## 1. Create New Habits

Since our brains establish habits, both good and bad, it can be tough to break out of them. Start new healthy habits that your brain and body will like. Why not have eggs for breakfast instead of the high fat donut? And start exercising over lunch break. Give it 30 days and your brain will crave these new healthy habits!

## 2. Don’t Ignore Your Cravings

If you neglect to answer all your cravings your brain will go haywire and you will crave even more. Create healthier swaps so your cravings do not hurt your waistline. Healthy snacking can help you to lose weight by actually boosting your metabolism all day long!

## 3. Think about Your Choices

Put your brain into action and think twice about the chocolate bar you are grabbing while checking out at the grocery store. Do you really need those extra calories? If you think twice you may change your mind and allow your brain to help you to lose weight and better your health!

## 4. Take Small Steps

Take small steps with changes and your mind will adjust to create new habits. These small steps will add up with great results on the scale and with a beautiful body you earned. And the best part is you will better your health in the process. Totally winning!

## 5. Eat at the Same Time

Since our minds like to have an established routine, eating at the same time each day will help you to lose weight. Just make sure you choose healthy food choices like grains, veggies and fruit. Then it will be a total win and your body will transform in the process!

## 6. Set and Achieve an Attainable Goal

There is nothing more rewarding then simply setting and achieving a goal. Go for the gusto by setting a small goal like fitting in a smaller pair of jeans. Now go out and smash this goal. You will be wowed by what you achieved and your brain will be happy feeling positively confident!

## 7. Be Positive

Keep your head up and think positive. Think positive in all aspects of your life and it will pay off. For example If and when you are thrown by food temptation at a party, you will seek and find the healthy choices. You will make the most out of every situation and achieve your goals through the process.

So think thin, be happy and make the most out of each and every day. You deserve to live a healthy and happy life and achieve your weight loss goals in the process!

# Delicious Recipe for Weightloss Coffee That'll Change Your Life ...

Here's a **recipe for weightloss coffee**. Not only does this boost metabolism, it makes more energy and is a potent fat burner. All you need to do is add 3 ingredients to your coffee! Simply drink this **recipe for weightloss coffee** without changing your lifestyle.

Put These 3 Ingredients In Your Coffee. After Just 2 Sips, Your Metabolism Will Be Faster Than Ever!

Published By: Healthy Life, October 21, 2016

Ingredients:
1 cup Coconut oil
1/2 tablespoon raw honey
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon cocoa (optional)

Directions:
Mix in a glass jar to keep in your fridge. Simply mix 2 Tsp. into your coffee every time you drink.

Instead of thinking of it as a diet, think of it as a new coffee creamer or a new coffee syrup.

The ingredients tend to be expensive. If you cannot afford them, scout out your grocery store sales. Ask a manager. Personally, I have found coconut oil everywhere from the vitamin section near the pharmacy to the salad dressing aisle!

Here is to a good cup of coffee and losing weight! Just in time for the New Year!

Cheers!

# 5 Ways to Diet without Annoying Your Friends ...

Are you dieting? If you are, then good for you. It takes 100% commitment and dedication to diet. For you to go for it, speaks so well of you. However, if you're out and about with your besties, you need some **ways to diet without annoying your friends.** Please don’t be the type of dieter that’s really irritating to be around with.

A post on Thrillist.com shows five types of annoying dieters. Truth be told, there are more than just five annoying dieters. The point is, don’t be like them. You just want to lose weight, not friends, right?

So as a committed and dedicated dieter, you might want to know more about these non-irritating ways to diet. After all, you wouldn’t want to get on anyone’s nerves. Here are the best **ways to diet without annoying your friends.**

## 1. Always Bring Your Own Food

You have to be responsible for own food and bringing it yourself is one of the top **ways to diet without annoying your friends**. You know what you need to eat. If you have decided to skimp on the carbs and the meats, then that’s your own choice. Just keep in mind that the whole world will not change for you. Just because you made such a choice, doesn’t mean everybody else has to follow.

So when you show up for a party, don’t expect the menu to be tailored to your diet because, you know what? It won’t be. The best thing you can do is to bring your own food. That way you won’t annoy the host. The last thing a host should have to do is to prepare a last minute menu for you.

## 2. Make Sure to Eat before Showing up

If you don’t want to bring your own food, then eat before showing up. That way, you won’t be tempted to eat whatever is there. You won’t end up ruining your diet or annoying anybody around.

Also, if you go out on a date, you won’t end up ordering the most expensive dish on the menu, simply because you’ve decided to eliminate meat from your diet. Unless you pay for own lobster or salmon dish, then go right ahead and order your anti-meat dish. However, you really shouldn’t compel your date to pay for your very expensive diet. That’s really going to annoy your date.

## 3. Avoid Being Condescending to Others Who Aren't Dieting

Just because you’re dieting, doesn’t mean everybody else has to. Always keep in mind that your diet is your own business. That means that what other people are eating is definitely none of your business.

So please avoid being too condescending to others who just want to enjoy their hamburgers and ice cream.

## 4. Avoid Talking Too Much about It

Instead of talking about your diet to every single person you come across, why don’t you just blog about it? That way, you can create an audience that will really appreciate you. But to constantly talk about your diet to every single person you come across with is really annoying. You might mention that you're trying to eat better, but let it go at that.

## 5. Get on a Complete Diet Program

This is probably the most non-irritating way for you to diet. There are a lot of diet programs that are very comprehensive and take care of your food and provide support.

Some diet programs even offer a buddy support system that can help you stay on track. A diet program is exactly what you need to get the kind of food you want and the kind of support you need to survive the whole process. Joining a diet program is also a good way for you to meet folks who are also in the same boat as you are.

Dieting is not easy. It can drive you nuts. Nonetheless, you need to consider the world around you. It won’t change simply because you’re dieting. But if you are on a diet program, keep it to yourself so you don't get annoying.

# Scrumptious Spiralized Salads for Weight Loss That Are Filling and Fun ...

If there is one piece of kitchen equipment I would advise you to have when you are trying to lose weight, it’s a spiralizer. Easy and fun to use, a spiralizer makes “spaghetti” from vegetables and fruit. If you’re craving pasta or just want your diet food to be more fun, the spiralizer is a godsend. You can find them on Amazon for under $20. Buy one and you’ll be making zoodles (zucchini noodles), coodles (cucumber noodles), voodles (veggie noodles) and froodles (fruit noodles) like there’s no tomorrow. And then you can make all these scrummy **spiralized salads**. Are you ready to be inspiralized?

# Cut Calories by Eating These Foil Packet Meals Today ...

The great thing about foil packet meals is that they are virtually mess free and you can use minimal cooking fats, saving you tons of calories. There are loads of things you can make in a foil packet and these are some wonderful ideas to get you started. Grill or bake the packets and you have delicious and nutritious meal that anyone will love.

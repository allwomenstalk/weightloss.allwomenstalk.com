# Genius Ways to Lose Baby Weight ...

Want to know some great ways to lose baby weight? I am going to tell you how I lost my weight since my baby's birth. There are certain things that helped me to drop the weight. Maybe this article will help women just to lose weight, not necessarily after pregnancy. Before I got pregnant I was a size 8. I was so happy being a size 8. During the pregnancy, I was focused on losing weight after pregnancy. I was really heavy. I lost 90% of this. Now I am confident to tell you how I lost my baby weight. I have been losing weight for about 3 months.
There are 5 easy ways to lose baby weight.

## 1. Diet

Before I could work out, some time had to pass. And it is a great way to be on a healthy diet. I have cut out takeaways and bad food. After 6 weeks you can start going to the gym. A healthy diet is one of the best ways to lose baby weight.

## 2. Gym

At the beginning, I went to the gym 3 times a week. If I don’t go to the gym, I run for 15 minutes to keep my fitness up. When I get home after the gym, I usually do weight exercises. I felt so much better. Do not avoid working out.

## 3. Smaller Portions

It is connected with food. You should watch food portions. You should eat more vegetables to fill you up for very few calories.

## 4. Focus on Inspiration

When I see the progress I can stop going so hard. It is difficult to continue. I just feel lazy. I don’t see other beautiful bodies. I just saw my pictures and wanted to get back to it. Sometimes people from Instagram can be helpful. It is a push for people.

## 5. Personal Trainer

It is really helpful to hire a personal trainer. I love my personal trainer. And I got to my size before pregnancy. It is pricey, but it is definitely worth it. If you cannot afford a personal trainer, there are many workouts online; you can get for free. You can do workouts at home instead of going to the gym. You can do whatever you like, running, walking, dancing etc. Now I am so happy to return back to my size.

# How the Sexy Ladies at Cosmo Cut Calories ...

The ladies over at [Cosmopolitan](https://www.cosmopolitan.com/health-fitness/a48654/hacks-for-making-your-food-healthier/) are beyond sexy. How did they get that way? By following these tips to cut calories:

## 1. Blot Oil off of Pizza

![Blot Oil off of Pizza](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n2/co/ms9ey3pj563b6caa1dcc3202720419_500x729.jpg)

You won't believe how much residue will end up on your napkin.

## 2. Eat Bagel Flats Instead of Full Bagels

![Eat Bagel Flats Instead of Full Bagels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k8/co/t0z004gu563b6caaa8c3c750869663_426x640.jpg)

This way, you won't be consuming as much unhealthy bread.

## 3. Order Your Own Appetizers

![Order Your Own Appetizers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x3/ia/ljs797iq563b6caac7c41046189433_500x667.jpg)

When you order appetizers you plan on sharing, you tend to get the unhealthy ones, so stop agreeing to split them.

## 4. Dress Your Food Yourself

![Dress Your Food Yourself](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f2/u9/qbln9pg2563b6cb540db4867340095_600x888.jpg)

Order the salad dressing on the side, so that they don't overload your food with it.

## 5. Eat Powdered Peanut Butter

![Eat Powdered Peanut Butter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xj/ef/jyrwrhfp563b6caaed2b8689938195_500x667.jpg)

This can taste as delicious as the peanut butter you're used to eating.

## 6. Eat White Fish Instead of Dark Fish

![Eat White Fish Instead of Dark Fish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mf/ru/bvpglitw563b6cab7d24f798970387_500x750.jpg)

Not all fish is created equally, you know.

## 7. Eat Cold Greens Instead of Warm Ones

![Eat Cold Greens Instead of Warm Ones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lj/ht/lzo6w8w7563b6cabaf98f421148818_500x569.jpg)

Stack some crisp lettuce onto your plate, ladies.

## 8. Opt for Plain, Dark Meat Poultry

![Opt for Plain, Dark Meat Poultry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a9/va/f1qnpmj3563b6cac38d1f551427672_499x750.jpg)

Do this instead of eating "light meat loaded with condiments."

## 9. Top Pasta and Pizza with Garlic Powder

![Top Pasta and Pizza with Garlic Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ko/ud/cl5lc7g9563b6cabd0f4a137957830_332x500.jpg)

This is healthier than Parmesan.

## 10. Pour off the Oil That Collects at the Top of Peanut Butter Jars and Dressing Bottles

![Pour off the Oil That Collects at the Top of Peanut Butter Jars and Dressing Bottles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wf/xe/gffbllpv563b6cac8d61c533506495_334x493.jpg)

This will get rid of tons of calories.

## 11. Use Unsweetened Almond Milk in Your Coffee

![Use Unsweetened Almond Milk in Your Coffee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ix/np/lvyftuzx563b6cac982ee325412428_475x708.jpg)

Swap your creamers and whole milk out for almond milk.

# 9 Excellent Tips for Slimmer Legs ...

Even though summer seems like a long way ahead, it's never too early to start looking up on tips for slimmer legs. It's all about preparation, guys! It'll pay off when the time comes along for you break into those super cute shorts. Besides, who wouldn't want slim legs all year long? Making others jealous because of them is quite fun too. Now there's some motivation! My tips for slimmer legs are in two categories - exercise and diet - so let's start with exercise...

## 1. Squats

I know we all dread them but it's one of the top tips for slimmer legs, trust me on this. Challenge yourselves with the month long Squat Challenge! By the end of the month, you'll have toned thighs and also a perky butt! Just imagine how good you'll look in those skinny jeans.

## 2. Lunges

It's not the most fun, I have to admit, but it's the absolute best thing to go with squats. Get the maximum out of your glutes workout by doing both! It doesn't take a lot of time, and you can basically do it wherever you want... I promise the soreness will pay off.

## 3. Run

There's just so many benefits to this - you can do it indoors on a treadmill or outdoors while getting in touch with nature - it's so versatile! Beginners can start out slow and steady. Don't stress yourselves out, there's plenty of really cool apps designed to help you start running. So lace up those running shoes, loves!

## 4. Dance

Ever noticed how dancers have amazing legs that just make you super green with envy? That's enough motivation to start getting your moves on. From ballet to street dancing, you're bound to find something you enjoy. Why not try out the latest Zumba trend?

## 5. Kickboxing

This is Adriana Lima's favorite exercises... need I say more? This intense exercise can burn up to 600 calories per hour! A little self defense won't hurt either. And now that we've covered exercise - whew! - let's move on to diet!

## 6. Eat

Do not starve. It will just slow down your metabolism. Opt for healthier alternatives or eat small meals every other hour instead. It won't just help you lose weight, it'll make you feel better about yourself. Yes, healthy food can actually make you happier so start munching down on some broccoli!

## 7. Drink Lots of Water

Water aids in digestion which will help with weight loss. It's also the best way for detox and to clear complexion. The general guideline would be to drink ½ ounce of water per pound body weight. So if you weigh 140 pounds, you should drink 70 ounces of water each day.

## 8. Stay Away from Highly Processed Food

Take some time to read nutrition labels before purchase, and not just the calories, guys! You'll be surprised to find out how much refined sugar and unhealthy fat it actually contains. Goodbye, curly fries. *Sobs* And if all else fails...

## 9. Wear High Heels

It's really the simplest way to slimmer legs. By making you look taller, it gives an illusion of a vertical stretch. It's a good way to tone those calves as well. Be sure to choose some comfy ones though; you won't want to end up with sore feet.

Remember, you can't just focus on one body part, it's an overall effect. But that's not a problem now, is it? So there you have it, guys! 9 Excellent Tips for Slimmer Legs! If there's any other more tricks and tips you'd like to share, feel free to comment! I'd love to know, and I'm sure others do too! Good luck, guys!

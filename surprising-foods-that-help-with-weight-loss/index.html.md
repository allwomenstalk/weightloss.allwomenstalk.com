# 10 Surprising Foods That Help with Weight Loss ...

There is no question that losing weight is difficult, and often it's hard to know where to start. Naturally, whatever you do, you need to incorporate exercise along with a good diet. However, it's also difficult to do a lot of exercise when you're first starting out. You might be carrying a few too many pounds to feel comfortable, in which case, you can begin by eating the right things to shed those extra lbs. If you think that might help you, then here are some surprising foods that help with weight loss.

## 1. Steak

Of the many surprising foods that help with weight loss, steak is one of the most misunderstood. Because it's a red meat, a lot of people think that it's bad for you. Now, if you're a vegetarian or a vegan, this one's not going to seem at all palatable, and that's perfectly all right. If you do eat red meat, however, don't be afraid that steak is going to totally break your diet. On the contrary, a lean cut of beef helps you lose weight because of the protein, which will ultimately help you build and keep essential muscle.

## 2. Oats

Oats are fantastic for losing weight because they help you feel full, so you'll eat less the rest of the day. This is because they are so full of fiber. Oats aren't like other carbs, because they take longer to digest. You can get more from both rolled oats and steel-cut oats.

## 3. Eggs

Eggs are a great food for weight loss as well – even the yolks! As long as the rest of your diet is low on calories, eating eggs can make you lose twice as much weight as you normally would. Again, the proteins are essential here. The proteins garnered from eggs will help keep you full throughout the rest of the day.

## 4. Blueberries

Blueberries are awesome for a number of reasons. They're high in antioxidants, for one thing, which is extremely beneficial. Not only that, but they have more antioxidants than any fruit, as well as plenty of fiber, which can prevent fat from getting absorbed. That, in turn, can lead to amazing weight loss.

## 5. Pomegranates

Pomegranates are also rich in antioxidants. This fruit has gotten increasingly popular lately, especially as a juice, but you can't forget about those tasty seeds. Since they have lots of fiber, they make a great alternative to sweets.

## 6. Almond Butter

A lot of surprising foods that help with weight loss are alternatives to the things you eat now. Almond butter is one of those. It makes a fantastic alternative spread, and can decrease the number of surges in blood sugar you have to go through. Thanks to that, almond butter can make you stay fuller longer.

## 7. Sardines

I have actually never eaten sardines, but I kind of want to try them. There's actually a reason to, since they can help you lose weight quite quickly. They've got tons of protein, not to mention omega-3 fatty acids. They have plenty of calcium as well, but they're incredibly low in mercury, which is highly important. Evidently, you can soak the little guys in milk for about an hour or so, and that will kill all that fishiness.

## 8. Chile Peppers

Chile peppers can help you lose a surprising amount of weight. This is primarily due to the fact that they improve your metabolism exponentially, mainly thanks to the amount of capsaicin they contain. The compound is thermogenic, so it helps your body burn calories for as much as twenty minutes after you've eaten the peppers. If you eat food containing chiles slowly, it will work even better – plus it keeps you from overeating.

## 9. Salmon

As you can see, many surprising foods that help with weight loss revolve around fish. You may not be a fan of sardines, but a lot of people love salmon. Even if you're not fond of it, there are many innovative ways to cook it that make it quite tasty. It's worth it to try, since the omega-3 fatty acids in salmon improve your body's sensitivity to insulin, which in turn will make it easier to lose weight and increase muscle.

## 10. Avocado

Guess what? It's totally okay to enjoy the guacamole! Avocados are high in fat, yes, but that's actually the reason it can help you lose weight. It's monounsaturated fat, which is incredibly good for your heart. Plus, it helps you stay full and satisfied for longer. Avocado makes a great substitute for sandwich spreads like mayonnaise.

As you can see, there are a lot of surprising foods that help with weight loss. In fact, there are many more than the ones listed here. Eating right can help jump start your weight loss, in addition to giving you the energy you need to start exercising regularly. What are some other healthy foods that you love to eat?

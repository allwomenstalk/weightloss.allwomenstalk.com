# Sip on These Low Calorie Hot Drinks Anytime ...

If you're anything like me, you want to hunker down with comfort food to keep you warm and cozy when the weather turns cold. You don't have to worry about gaining weight though because there are lots of great drink options that will keep you from overdoing it on fat and calories. Just remember that you have to remember to balance your liquid calories with what you eat for the day to keep things in balance. Here are some fall-worthy hot drinks that you'll crave all the time. Without any of the guilt.

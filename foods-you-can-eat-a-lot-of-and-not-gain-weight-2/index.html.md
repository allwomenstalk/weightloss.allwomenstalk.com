# Foods You Can Eat a Lot of and Not Gain Weight ...

After viewing this video on **foods you can eat a lot of and not gain weight**, I took into consideration the costs of the food.

Healthy foods always get a bad rap. However, if you do your research, you CAN afford them. The video had 22 suggestions. Many were quite costly. So, I am going to share with you what I think are affordable...

Eggs
Cucumber
Salads
Boiled potatoes
Broccoli
Cauliflower
Oatmeal
Broth-based soups
Cottage cheese

So, pay attention to your local grocery store's sales. Although, even if not on sale, the groceries I have selected are still going to be cheap. Study some new recipes so you do not get bored.

Good luck!

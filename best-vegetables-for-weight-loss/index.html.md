# 5 Best Vegetables for Weight Loss for Girls Trying to Lose a Few Pounds ...

As you ask yourself the following series of questions, also ask what are the **best vegetables for weight loss**? Women often ask themselves, what is the best diet to lose weight fast? Is there any particular regimen I can follow to see results soon? Weight loss can be a challenge, but with proper exercise and diet you can easily lose weight. Include these 5 **best vegetables for weight loss** in your daily meals and you will see results sooner than you think.

## 1. Onions

![dish, food, cuisine, vegetarian food, vegetable,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ze/lq/ze8tg4j359ae584de0024398009952_600x749.jpg)

Would you be surprised to find that onions are one of the **best vegetables for weight loss.** Onions are low in calories and easily kill fat. They can be roasted, grilled, or sautéed, and add the best flavor to your meals.

## 2. Cucumber

![vegetable, dish, vegetarian food, food, cucumber,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cy/z8/kudxmqk259ae582929618104242538_600x466.jpg)

Cucumbers are mainly water based and help reduce hunger. When you feel like you want to eat something, have some slices of cucumber to help reduce your appetite. Cucumbers also help get rid of toxins making you feel instantly lighter and better.

## 3. Leafy Greens

![dish, spinach salad, salad, food, vegetable,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hs/k5/g3c29jue59ae5a4fe3559668764425_600x750.jpg)

Leafy greens are very important, especially if you want to meet your daily mineral and vitamin intake. Cabbage, broccoli, lettuce, and spinach are among the best. You must make sure that you eat enough of these foods on a regular basis. Luckily, they taste good, so it shouldn't be a problem.

## 4. Pumpkin

![dish, food, vegetable, vegetarian food, cuisine,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/up/a9/nx6w193s59aec920aeeef301707361_600x751.jpg)

Pumpkin is important as well because it is rich in vitamin C, and can help you keep your energy levels high. It can also prevent weight gain. Enjoy it in stew or chili for something new and delicious.

## 5. Green Pepper

![natural foods, vegetable, local food, produce, food,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z6/w9/p1knnxd359ae5b93d90f5927658597_600x479.jpg)

Green peppers, and any others that are part of the capsicum genus, can help you decrease the amount of fat in your body and will boost your metabolism as well. Eating this veggie will make you burst with the energy! Enjoy it in salads or soups.

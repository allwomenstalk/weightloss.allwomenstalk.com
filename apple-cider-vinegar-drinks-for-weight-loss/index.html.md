## 1. Raspberry Apple Cider Vinegar Drink

Ingredients:
2 tbsp. raspberries
1 tbsp. ACV
2 tbsp. lemon juice
2 tbsp. maple syrup
Water
Ice cubes

Directions:
Add raspberries and maple syrup to the glass. Mush with spoon to juice. Add ACV, lemon juice and ice cubes. Fill with water. Drink.

## 2. Maple Syrup Vinegar Soda

Ingredients:
1 1/2 tbsp. maple syrup
1 1/2 tbsp. ACV
1/4 tsp. vanilla extract
Cool sparkling water
Ice cubes

Directions:
In a large glass, mix maple syrup, ACV and vanilla. Fill with sparkling water. Add a few ice cubes.

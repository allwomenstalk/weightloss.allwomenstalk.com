# 31 Decadent Chopped Salads to Help You Lose Weight ...

When you want to lose weight, you must burn more calories than you consume. That makes a chopped salad the perfect choice. Not only are veggies low in calories, but they are high in fiber, which helps you feel full for longer. If you choose a recipe that contains cheese, bacon or other extras, make sure you balance the calorie content with what you eat for the rest of the day so that you don;t overdo it.

# 7 Ways to Eat Your Fave Summer Treats While Still Losing Weight ...

**Weight loss** and enjoying your favorite summer treats-sounds like an oxymoron, right? Yet, here we are in 2024, and it turns out you really can have your ice cream and eat it too. Imagine lounging on a sandy beach, popsicle in hand, while still feeling great about your health goals. Sounds like a dream come true, yes? In this article, we'll explore the ultimate guide to enjoying delicious, seasonal goodies without tipping the scales.

Now, I've been down this road myself. You wouldn’t believe how many diets I’ve tried, from that _kale smoothie cleanse_ to the infamous _cabbage soup diet_. None of them offered me both the joy of eating and the promise of staying in shape during those glorious summer months. Well, my friends, it’s a new era!

You'll be ecstatic to learn about healthier _alternatives_, smarter portion control, and even mind tricks that can help you savor every bite without falling into a food coma. Let's dive deeper into these [ingenious tips](#1) that can skyrocket your summer-deliciously!

-
**Mindful eating**: Have you ever eaten an entire ice cream cone without truly tasting it? I certainly have. Mindful eating is about being present, savoring every lick of that creamy goodness, and relishing the _texture_ and flavor that make summer treats so irresistible.

-
Choosing **sugar-free** options: Believe me, not all sugar-free treats taste like cardboard. From sugar-free sorbets to naturally sweetened fruit popsicles, there’s a world of options that can please your palate and keep your blood sugar in check.

-
Portion control magic: This might sound like an evil trick, but trust me, it works. Read about these secret [portion control hacks](#2) that make you feel like you’re indulging without actually overdoing it.

-
Balancing treats with exercise: Ah, the old 'burn what you eat' doctrine. It's all about routines, as detailed in [balancing calories with activity](#3). Enjoy a treat and then try a fun activity-it could be anything from a breezy evening walk to a high-energy game of beach volleyball.

-
Incorporating more **protein**: Sometimes a scoop of Greek yogurt with fresh berries can satisfy your sweet tooth while packing in that much-needed protein. This combo can keep you feeling full and help you avoid that dreaded sugar crash.

-
Avoiding empty calories: If you’re going to indulge, make sure it’s worth it! Opt for treats that have some nutritional value, making each calorie count toward something beneficial for your body.

-
_Refreshing drinks_: Hydrate and indulge at the same time by exploring delicious, low-calorie drink options. Think iced herbal teas, or a homemade lemonade with a twist!

Ready to dive in and make your summer not just hot but oh-so-healthy? Let’s explore these seven innovative ways to keep the flavor of summer alive while looking and feeling our best. Cheers to a fun, fit, and fulfilling summer!

## 1. Portion Control

![Portion Control](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q0/um/ppdc4scf574b48a9447a8172163368_525x525.jpg)

Your daily visit to the ice cream shop does not necessarily have to stop just for you to lose weight, because with proper portion control you can enjoy in moderation. There is the small, medium, large and oh yeah, the kid’s size. The kid’s version of ice cream is no more than a few teaspoons and as a result will not impact your weight loss. So enjoy in moderation!

## 2. Enjoy Summer Veggies

![Enjoy Summer Veggies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nq/eg/pmzfsbxs574b491bb24c2856999606_600x600.jpg)

Summer squash, basil, cucumbers, tomatoes and the endless array of summer veggies will not only help you to slim down but you will also better your health. So make sure you include all these veggies in your summer eating plan. You can chop those mentioned and even make a fresh summer salad!

## 3. Fruit Salad

Watermelon, cantaloupe, nectarines, peaches, blueberries and the countless other fruits for summer can certainly fulfill any sweet tooth, that is for sure! Make a fruit salad to keep in your refrigerator so when hunger strikes you are ready to go. You can still lose weight, provide your body with healthy antioxidant-filled fruit that can fight off cancer and you will feel great!

## 4. Make Summer Smoothies

![Make Summer Smoothies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kr/lb/rpr9zdpj574b4837cd23c770016425_600x600.jpg)

Craving a sweet treat that will never disappoint? Then make a summer smoothie with all the summer veggies and fruits you love. Add a few tablespoons of greek yogurt to get the smooth, creamy texture you will be sure to love! Sip, relax and enjoy!

## 5. Use the Grill

![Use the Grill](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g1/ie/c62wqo61574b49c16efae111423220_600x600.jpg)

Eat lean and enjoy while you grill this summer with your fave veggies and lean protein. Diversify your meals by getting creative each day. Healthy marinades like balsamic, Italian and even teriyaki can go a long way in adding to the taste. So enjoy as you slim down this summer and never ever feel deprived!

## 6. Create Healthy Swaps

![Create Healthy Swaps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lb/nx/y0e91rai574b4b7728605519368099_600x600.jpg)

Gone are the days of fried food, because you are on the right track to a healthier and happier lifestyle. But good news, you can still enjoy summer faves you love, like breaded zucchini - just have the healthier swap. Bread the zucchini with whole wheat bread crumbs and bake in the oven or skip the breading and just go to the grill.

## 7. Make Sure You Get Your Workouts in Daily

![Make Sure You Get Your Workouts in Daily](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/te/eo/f0ir2oqu574b48fc18533347626851_600x600.jpg)

Consistency will be key to achieving the great results you deserve, so make sure you get your workouts in every single day! If you want to enjoy your summer treats and still lose weight, you need to ensure your activity level is high, so get up and get moving. Every step you take will bring you one step closer to your goal!

So enjoy your summer treats, be mindful of making healthy choices and enjoy each and every bite!

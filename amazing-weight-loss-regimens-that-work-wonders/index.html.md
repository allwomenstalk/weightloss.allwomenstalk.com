# Amazing Weight Loss Regimens That Work Wonders ...

It’s the beginning of a new year, which means that everyone is trying out a new weight loss regimen. It can be tough, but if you stick with it, the results will be so worth it! If you’re looking for a new weight loss regimen to try out, look no further than this list. Some are just workouts, some are just meal plans, and some are both. Tailor what works with your lifestyle and get started on a great new journey of health and fitness! It’s not going to be an easy road, but I believe that you can get there and you will get there!

## 1. 21 Day Fix

![human action, clothing, person, image, lady,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ao/xg/twiotm1958695a552f8f1754371135_600x600.jpg)

I’m just over halfway through the 21 Day Fix, and it’s one of my favorite workout and diet regimens I’ve ever done. I’m never hungry, and there aren’t a lot of foods that are off limits. Plus, after a few days, you really don’t want those foods that are off limits! The workouts are also great and perfect for the people who don’t have a lot of time.

## 2. Insanity

![clothing, swimwear, undergarment, muscle, g string,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ex/ff/kg9kmwgj58695a73d91bc646388865_600x600.jpg)

I’ve never done this workout regimen, but from what I’ve heard, it’s killer and it works. Isn’t that what we all want in the end? If you’re looking for a workout that’s extremely intense, this is definitely the one to do!

## 3. Fat, Sick, and Nearly Dead Juice Cleanse

![hair, clothing, person, lingerie, undergarment,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zr/xu/kj9ogag358695a8961c1b843864153_600x749.jpg)

I did this juice cleanse a few months ago, and I really loved it, but it’s hard to keep up. I have a lot of digestive issues, so I loved this for giving my digestive system break. Yes, you lose a weight without exercising, but you can’t live on a juice cleanse for the rest of your life, and eventually you’ll want food again!

## 4. Weight Watchers

![human action, person, structure, barechestedness, human positions,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jx/ja/jdrz2gct58695a9f82855747182422_600x600.jpg)

There’s a reason that this program has been along for as long as it has. It works! If you’re looking for a program that’s guaranteed to work, Weight Watchers is definitely one to try. Plus, it’s recently been backed and supported by Oprah. Who doesn’t want to be like Oprah?

## 5. Atkins

![hair, person, clothing, muscle, mouth,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g2/tq/qu6f8hpw58695abe9ce47707771587_600x600.jpg)

If you’re someone who doesn’t like carbs, this might be a better alternative for you than Weight Watchers. However, just like Weight Watchers, it’s a program that’s been around for a long time because it works! If you’re looking for a program that will give you the results you want, you must try Atkins!

## 6. Paleo Diet

![active undergarment, clothing, undergarment, underpants, muscle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qg/pq/o6kynqz758695aee1acf9536941576_600x600.jpg)

The paleo diet is a newer diet that people are loving. It’s similar to Atkins, but it’s more based on foods that we assume “cavemen” ate thousands of years ago. That means that you eat mostly meat, fish, vegetables and fruit. If this is something that looks like a food plan you’d like, you should definitely try this, because the weight will fall right off!

## 7. Nutrisystem

![clothing, underpants, active undergarment, undergarment, muscle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vm/fp/fv71csul58695b01ef904717326542_400x400.jpg)

I’m not usually someone who likes prepackaged food sent to me. I prefer to eat fresh food that I’ve prepared myself. However, there are a ton of people who love when food shows up at their door just because it’s convenient, and it’s a diet that works! If you’re going to try one, try Nutrisystem for meals delivered right to your door!

## 8. Jenny Craig

![clothing, active undergarment, undergarment, underpants, muscle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hx/kx/s5vjoihu587089bf559c3692644972_600x600.jpg)

There's a reason so many celebrities have endorsed this plan for so many years. From convenient check ins, to meals delivered to your door, Jenny Craig is simple to follow, delicious, and easy!

## 9. Slimfast

![clothing, person, lady, muscle, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yy/es/bpsrrmix587089eb89ae2365211197_600x674.jpg)

Quick, reasonably priced, and great for the girl on the go! Slimfast plans are as simple as 1-2-3 (just like their slogan says) 1 meal of 500 calories, 2 meals replaced with shakes, and 3 snacks is all it takes to be successful.

## 10. Cize

![hair, clothing, active undergarment, thigh, human positions,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a7/o9/a7wmawhh58708a610e46a275669327_600x742.jpg)

Dance meets exercise in this Beachbody workout routine. Follow the 30 day meal plan options and exercise routine and see results! It's fun, fit for any fitness level, and you'll truly see results and enjoy! It won't feel like working out when you do cize.

# 6 Bedtime Hacks to Lose Weight ...

When you think about the process of losing weight, the first things that come to your mind are probably going to the gym to work ou,t and committing to a strict calorie controlled diet, but did you know that you can actually contribute to dropping those extra pounds even as you sleep? I’m talking about bedtime and the late evening that you spend at home; you don’t have to be in the gym to work towards your goal weight. Here are six **bedtime hacks to lose weight. **

## 1. No Food after 7PM

Have dinner early in the evening and then be strict with yourself and refrain from eating more food after 7pm. Food takes a considerable amount of time to break down within the body, and it can’t happen efficiently if you are asleep in your bed as it happens. Give yourself a good three hours for the digestion process to occur before hitting the pillow.

## 2. Me Time

Take some special time to focus on de-stressing every evening, because you would be amazed by how much your anxieties and worries can impact your snack craving feelings of hunger. The calmer and more relaxed you can be in the evening through to bedtime, the less likely you will be to indulge in unhealthy treats.

## 3. Warm Milk

Get into the habit of drinking a glass of warm milk before you go to bed. Milk contains a type of protein called casein that is helpful in building and sustaining lean muscle in the body, and it has the added benefit of boosting your metabolism to achieve higher rates of calorie burning even as you sleep.

## 4. Room Lighting

You need your room to be as dark as possible in order to have the best nights’ sleep. Studies have shown that women who sleep in darker conditions tend to be a healthier weight, the reason believed to be because of the more successful production of melatonin in the body that darker conditions support.

## 5. Prepare Lunch and Snacks

Right before bedtime, make yourself a healthy packed lunch with a few daytime snacks included. This will save you waking up in the morning and being too sleepy to do so, which leads on to saving you from spending money on some junk food when you get to work!

## 6. Meal Plan

Take all of the chances of binge eating before bedtime away by finding a good meal plan and sticking to it. It can be difficult to get used to at first, but it will soon become a habit and something that you don’t even think about. Plan your meals out on Sunday night and you will feel much better about your food consumption all week long.

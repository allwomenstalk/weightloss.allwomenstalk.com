# 10 Best Foods  for a Flat Belly All Year Long ...

Ladies, do you already have a large stomach and finally want to take a step in the right direction to do something about it? You need the **best foods for a flat belly.** On the other hand, do you have a slim belly and want to prevent gaining a big one? The following are the **best foods for a flat belly** that will be your new BFF in the destruction of belly fat!

## 1. Canned Tuna in Water

Tuna is high in protein and basically fat-free. However, you should only eat it twice a week, due to high levels of mercury. Tuna is one of the **best foods for a flat belly,** but if you don't like fish, there are lots of other choices.

## 2. Asparagus

This is a good source of fiber, keeping you full, and is a good diuretic for the removal of water and waste from your body.

## 3. Bananas

A resistant starch carb, bananas make you feel full, boost your metabolism and decreases bloating.

## 4. Cucumbers

Cucumbers prevent bloating and reduce swelling due to their content of quercetin, an antioxidant.

## 5. Eggs

Mega-efficient in satisfying hunger, you're going to love eggs. Amino acids help build and preserve lean muscle mass, while the choline stokes fat burning.

## 6. Turkey

How easy is a sandwich? Turkey is high in protein and keeps your insulin levels low after meals due to leucine, which keeps the fat off of your belly.

## 7. Whey Protein Isolate

It's as easy as shake, shake, shake! The teaming up of calcium and branched chained amino acids burn fat.

## 8. Low-Fat Chocolate Milk

After a workout, have a cup of chocolate milk for the for carbs and protein to refuel, gain muscle and lose fat.

## 9. Mushrooms

Mushrooms are full of Vitamin D, which helps keep belly fat at bay.

## 10. Beans

Low in calories and high in filling fiber, beans are tasty and helpful. Research has proven that bean eaters have slimmer waistlines and weigh less.

Not only are these foods easy to find, they are affordable and easy to stock up on as staples for your kitchen.

# The Best Weight\-Loss Iced Tea Recipe Ever ...

This is the most simple way on **how to make the best weight-loss iced tea recipe** ever!

There is really nothing hard about her method of making this **iced tea**!

Stick a tea bag in a pitcher, fill with water, stick in the fridge overnight and wake-up to fresh tea!

Is it a sunny day? Place your pitcher near a window. No need to even go outside!

Personally, I would use more than one tea bag. I use 10 tea bags and lemon juice.

Is not her recipe the simplest way to make iced tea?

Have this unsweetened iced tea just as if you are dining at a restaurant! Oh yeah, when you make the iced tea yourself, no need to be embarrassed by all of the re-fills either!
